Frequently Asked Questions
To register for a new account, go to SpartanCounselor.com and click register. You will need a Sjsu.edu email in order to register.
In order to change profile content, click the MyProfile tab on the left side of the screen, and then click the eidt button. It should then allow you to edit the content for your profile.
To view your prifle and content, click the MyProfile tab on the left side of the screen and it will direct you to your profile.
In order to search for a counselor, click the CounselorSearch tab on the left side of the screen, and it will direct you to the counselor search page. From there you can search for a counselor by entering the counsleor name or by clicking the view all counslors button to view on the counslor registered.
In order to chat with a counsleor. Go to the CounsleorSearch tab and search for the counselor you like. Click the name of the counselor and it will direct you to the counselors profile page, and from there you can click the direct message button. After you have established a chat, you can then click the chat tab on the left side of the screen in order to continue the chat.
